Widget Integration

Display our beautiful nature widget on your website in just two simple steps. The process is quick, secure, and requires no advanced technical skills.

1 Add the Widget Script

Copy the following code block and paste it just before the closing </body> tag of your webpage. Important: You must customize the values marked in the example below to match your brand:

Customize These Values:

  • data-api-key: Your unique API key (find it in API Access)
  • data-name: Replace "BRAND" with YOUR brand or company name
  • data-primary-color: Use your website's main color (hex format: #RRGGBB)
  • data-secondary-color: Use your website's accent color for better integration
  • data-font-family: Optional - Use your website's font (e.g., "Arial", "Roboto", "Poppins")
<script
    src="../widget/widget.js"
    data-api-key="YOUR_API_KEY_HERE"
    data-name="BRAND"
    data-primary-color="#0ea5a4"
    data-secondary-color="#7c3aed"
    data-font-family="Roboto"
>
</script>
How to find your colors: Use your browser's developer tools (F12) to inspect your website and copy the exact hex codes of your brand colors. This ensures perfect visual integration with your existing design.
Performance Note: This script loads the widget asynchronously without affecting your site's performance. Ensure it's placed before the closing </body> tag for optimal loading.
2 Add a Button or Link to Open the Widget

To allow your users to open the widget, you can use either a button or a link on your website. Both will open the widget when they call the openNatureWidget() function included in the widget script.
