Widget Placement Strategy

Where you place your widget on your website significantly impacts how many times it gets clicked.

Best Pages to Add the Widget

  • Product/Service Pages: Show related destinations alongside your offerings
  • Destination Pages: Let visitors explore similar places
  • Booking Pages: Help customers find additional destinations
  • Blog Posts: Enhance travel-related content with the widget

Pages to Avoid

  • Generic homepage (unless integrated naturally)
  • Error or "not found" pages
  • Pages unrelated to travel or destinations
  • Low-traffic pages
Placement Tip

Place the widget where visitors have high intent - when they're actively looking for travel information, not just browsing casually.

Creating Great Custom Locations

Your custom locations drive conversions. Make them compelling!

High-Quality Images

  • Use professional photos (at least 1600x1200 pixels)
  • Landscape format (4:3 ratio) works best
  • Show the best features of the location
  • Avoid small or low-resolution images

Compelling Descriptions

Write descriptions that make people want to visit:

  • Include specific features and highlights
  • Mention what makes it unique
  • Be honest about what visitors will experience
  • Keep it concise but informative

Always Include Pricing

Transparent pricing builds trust and increases bookings:

  • Show clear per-person or per-night pricing
  • Use "From" for variable pricing
  • Match your audience's currency
  • Update prices seasonally if they change

Direct Booking Links

Link directly to booking pages, not your homepage:

  • Link to the specific location or package
  • Include booking discounts in the link if available
  • Test that links work before saving
Conversion Boost

Well-created custom locations with high-quality images and direct links convert 3x better than basic listings.

Managing Your Costs

Stay within budget and optimize your plan for maximum value.

Understanding "Openings"

Only when a visitor clicks the widget button does it count as 1 opening.

  • Widget loading on a page = free (unlimited)
  • Visitor clicks to open widget = 1 opening
  • Browsing inside the widget = free
  • Clicking a destination link = free

Using Analytics to Improve Results

Your analytics dashboard shows where your clicks come from and which destinations are most popular.

Key Metrics to Track

  • Total Opens: How many times your widget was opened
  • Popular Locations: Which destinations get the most interest
  • Top Countries: Where your visitors are from
  • Performance by Domain: Which of your websites drives more clicks

How to Use This Data

Use your insights to drive better results:

  • Add more custom locations in high-demand categories
  • Place widgets on your best-performing pages
  • Update pricing for popular destinations
  • Focus marketing efforts on top-converting regions
  • Remove underperforming custom locations
Weekly Review

Spend 15 minutes weekly reviewing your analytics. Regular optimization can increase conversions by 20-40%.

Optimization Tips

These proven tactics improve widget performance:

Customize the Button Text

Use compelling button text that matches your brand:

  • "Explore Destinations"
  • "Book Your Adventure"
  • "Find Inspiration"
  • "Discover More Places"

Use Multiple Widgets Strategically

  • Main widget on homepage/general pages
  • Country widget on region-specific pages
  • Single-place widget on detail pages

Multiple Custom Locations

  • Create at least 5-10 custom locations
  • Keep them updated with current pricing
  • Rotate featured locations seasonally
  • Focus on high-margin offerings

A/B Testing

Test different placements and messaging:

  • Try different button text
  • Test placement on different pages
  • Experiment with widget styling
  • Track which variations perform best

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Learn from others' mistakes:

Mistake 1: Poor Quality Custom Locations

Problem: Low-resolution images, vague descriptions, no pricing

Solution: Invest in professional images and detailed, compelling descriptions with transparent pricing.

Mistake 2: Wrong Page Placement

Problem: Widget on pages where visitors aren't looking for travel

Solution: Place widgets on travel-relevant pages where visitors have high intent.

Mistake 3: Not Monitoring Analytics

Problem: Set it and forget it - missing optimization opportunities

Solution: Check analytics weekly and continuously optimize your strategy.

Mistake 4: Ignoring Usage Limits

Problem: Widget stops working when you exceed your monthly limit

Solution: Monitor usage and upgrade your plan proactively before hitting limits.
