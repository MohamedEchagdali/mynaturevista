Welcome to MyNaturevista

Your complete guide to adding interactive nature destination widgets to your website

What is MyNaturevista?

MyNaturevista is a service that lets you add a beautiful interactive widget to your website. This widget allows your visitors to explore amazing nature destinations around the world.

Simple to Use

You get a small code snippet to add to your website. That's it! Your visitors can then click to explore destinations without leaving your site.

What the Widget Does

The widget is a single interactive window that shows:

  • Countries - A list of countries with beautiful nature destinations
  • Places - When visitors click a country, they see specific places to visit
  • Details - Click any place to see photos, descriptions, and visit information

Your visitors navigate through these three sections inside the widget - it's like a mini travel guide on your website!

Quick Start in 3 Simple Steps

1

Choose Your Plan

Select a subscription plan that matches your website's traffic. Start with Starter if you're unsure.

2

Get Your Widget Code

Create an API key in the dashboard and copy your unique widget code.

3

Add to Your Website

Paste the code into your website before the closing body tag. Done!
