Create and Manage API Keys

Your API key is how you connect your website to MyNaturevista. Each key is unique and can be limited to specific websites for security.

Creating a New API Key

1

Click API Access

In the left sidebar, click "API Access"

2

Create New Key

Click the "Generate API Key" button

3

Save

Click Generate. Your API key will be created and displayed on the screen

Editing an API Key

You can edit which domains are allowed to use each API key. This is useful if you want to add a new domain or remove an old one.

1

Click API Access

Go to "API Access" in the sidebar

2

Manage Domain & API Key

Each domain has its own container where you can create, revoke, or delete the API key and manage the domain settings.

3

Update Domains

Add or remove website domains and save

Tip: Different Keys Per Website

Create a separate API key for each website you own. This makes it easier to track which site gets more clicks.

Account Settings

Manage your account security, data privacy, and account lifecycle.

Available Account Actions

1

Change Your Password

You can update your password at any time to keep your account secure.

2

Request Your Data (GDPR)

You can request a copy of your personal data, which will be sent to your registered email in compliance with GDPR regulations.

3

Delete Your Account

You may permanently delete your account. This action is irreversible.

4

Update Additional Information

For changes to other account details, please contact our support team for assistance.

Billing & Plans

Manage your subscription, plans, and billing information directly from the Billing section in the sidebar.

Billing Section (Sidebar)

The Billing section in the sidebar allows you to review your financial activity and subscription details.

  • View your current subscription plan
  • Access your complete invoice history
  • Download invoices in PDF format
  • See the total amount paid

View or Upgrade Your Plan

From the Billing section, you can view your current plan and choose to upgrade if needed.

1

Open Billing

Click on "Billing" in the sidebar navigation.

2

Select a Plan

Review your current plan or select a new one that fits your usage.

3

Complete Payment

Follow the secure checkout process to confirm your upgrade.

Invoices & Payment History

You can view all your invoices, download them, and track your total payments from the Billing section.

Need Help?

If you have any billing questions or need assistance, please contact our support team.

Click Add Domain

2

Complete Purchase

Follow the secure checkout process

3

Create an API Key

Go to "API Access" and create a new key for your new domain

Custom Locations

Add your own locations (hotels, restaurants, attractions, etc.) to showcase alongside natural destinations.

Create a Custom Location

1

Click Custom Locations

In the Admin Widget section, click "Custom Locations"

2

Click Add New

Click "Add New Location"

3

Fill in Details

Add title, description, image, price, location coordinates, and booking link

4

Save

Click Save Place. Your location will appear in the widget

Best Practices for Custom Locations

  • Use high-quality images (at least 1600x1200 pixels)
  • Include clear, compelling descriptions
  • Always show pricing for transparency
  • Provide a direct booking or contact link
  • Use accurate location coordinates

Export Your Data

You can download all your data for backup or transfer to another service.

How to Export Your Data

1

Go to Account Settings

Click "Account Settings" in the Account section

2

Export Data

Scroll down and click "Data Request"

3

Check Your Email

We'll email your data file within 24 hours

Delete Account

You can delete your account at any time. This action is permanent.

Warning: Account Deletion is Permanent

When you delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed. This includes:

  • Your account and profile information
  • All API keys and custom locations
  • Your analytics history
  • Active subscription will be cancelled

How to Delete Your Account

1

Go to Account Settings

Click "Account Settings"

2

Click Delete Account

Scroll to the bottom and click "Delete Account"

3

Confirm

Confirm the deletion when prompted
